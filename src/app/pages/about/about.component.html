Hospital History

Founded in 1995, SmartQueue Hospital has grown from a small medical facility to a comprehensive healthcare institution serving over 100,000 patients annually. Our commitment to excellence and patient care has made us one of the most trusted hospitals in the region.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a team of highly skilled medical professionals, and a patient-centered approach, we continue to set new standards in healthcare delivery.
